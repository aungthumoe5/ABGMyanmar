About Us

ABG Group is a strong group of farmers, rice mill owners and rice trading individuals and companies, as well as fishery and livestock, from the regions of East Yangon, Thongwa and Kayan Townships.

Our Farmers

Rice growing is lifetime experience expertise of ABG farmers. 4000 acres of farms create a strong supply of paddy to ABG rice mills.

Our Rice Mills

ABG rice mills have a total output capacity of 300 MT/day.

Our Trading

Thongwa Shwe Wah Myay manages trading of rice, both locally and internationally. Our rice specialist company's management team is composed of farmers, rice mill owners, rice traders and businessmen in the rice industry.

Our Fishery and Livestock

ABG fish farms are situated at Thet Kal Kone Wa Fishery zone near Kayan Township. The main species we are growing is ROHU and we currently have a farm area of 250 acres. Cow farms are being tested and at the initial phase of growing currently.
