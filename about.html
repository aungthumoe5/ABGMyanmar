About Us

We are a strong group of farmers, rice mill owners and rice trading individuals and companies, as well as fishery and livestock, from the regions of East Yangon, Thongwa and Khayan Townships.

Our Farmers

Growing of rice is lifetime experience expertise of ABG farmers. 4000 acres of farms create a strong supply of paddy to ABG rice mills.

Our Rice Mills

ABG rice mills have a total capacity of 300MT/day.

Our Trading

Thongwa Shwe Wah Myay manages trading of rice, both locally and internationally. Our rice specialist company's managing team includes farmers of big scale, rice mill owners, top rice traders and businessmen in the rice industry.

Our Fishery and Livestock

ABG fish farms are situated at Thet Kal Kone Wa Fishery zone near Khayan Township. The main species we are growing is ROHU and 250 Acres of fish farms produces XXXXXXMT of ROHU annually. Cow farms are being tested and at the initial phase of growing currently.
