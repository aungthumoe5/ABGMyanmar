Paw San Rice

Paw San is white long grain rice of Myanmar. Because of its looks, it is also known as "Pearl Paw San" rice. Paw San was awarded Worlds best rice at the World Rice Conference 2011 held in Ho Chi Minh, Vietnam. Paw San is very special, excellent in eating quality, soft texture with very delightful fragrance.

Paw San might soon become famous in the international market. It is famous for its elongation characteristics (Up to three times bigger than original size after cooking), as well as for its nutritional benefits and great taste.
